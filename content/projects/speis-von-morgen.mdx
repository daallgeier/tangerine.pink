As a collective of people who care about the environment and the food they eat, we want to make it easy for you to buy local, organic, and sustainable food. We are a local grocery store in Innsbruck, Austria and sell organic food and products from local farmers and producers. Our goal is to provide you with the best quality products at affordable prices. We also offer a wide range of vegan and vegetarian options. You can find us at our store next to the Cinema. We hope to Metropol Cinnema in the centre of Innsbruck see you soon!
